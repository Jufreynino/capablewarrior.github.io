OUR CLIENT ADVANTAGE




HIGHLY QUALIFIED GUARDS

The Agency, in general, adopts the requirements of R.A 5487 as the minimum standard for the qualifications of guards. Clients, nonetheless, have the right and prerogative to establish qualification of guards to be assigned in their premises

TRAINED & LICENSED SECURITY GUARDS

We give high premium on training. Our guards undergo a well-rounded and thorough training in all aspects of security work before they are given assignments. The minimum requirement of a guard is at least high school graduate, or has earned not less than 72 units in college.

NO UNION TIE-UP

Total quality service is foremost of our commitments to our clients. We offer a healthy working environment and relationship among our guards, the agency and the clients, free form work interruption such as but not limited to work stoppage, strikes and other labor related problems and concern
